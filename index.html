Why Central Bank Rates Matter

Policy rates are one of the most important drivers of government bond yields. Changes in central bank policy can affect borrowing costs, yield curves, inflation expectations and macroeconomic conditions.

How to Use This Tracker

Use this dashboard to compare monetary policy settings across economies, identify where policy is tighter or looser, and connect central bank trends with sovereign bond markets.
